Services

UI/UX Design

One of my specialties is designing intuitive and visually appealing user experiences using Figma. From wireframes and interactive prototypes to polished interfaces, I create user-centered designs that are functional, engaging, and tailored to both business goals and user needs.

Branding

I have experience creating strong and memorable brand identities. With extensive knowledge of Adobe Illustrator, I design logos, brand guidelines, and other cohesive visual elements and advertising materials that effectively communicate a brand’s personality, values, and vision. My goal is always to combine creativity with strategic design to deliver visuals that not only look great but also leave a lasting impact.

3D

I have experience working with Blender and ZBrush to create 3D models and digital artwork. I enjoy learning new techniques and challenging myself with creative projects that help me grow as a designer. From modeling and sculpting to basic texturing and rendering, I focus on developing high-quality work while continuously improving my skills and understanding of 3D design.
